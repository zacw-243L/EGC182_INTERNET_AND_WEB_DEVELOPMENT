For a taste of Europe in Bishan, look no further than the 2nd floor of Junction 8 shopping centre. Here you’ll find one of Ambush’s cosiest venues, which is the perfect setting for the fabulous pan-European comfort food being served up: sizzling French and German sausages, fragrant Spanish paella, and succulent Farmhouse Lamb Shank are just some of the favourites which our customers come back for time and time again.
